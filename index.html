About the game

A sharper spin on classic snake

Slither N' Slash is an independent arcade game built by Postak Creations. It combines the familiar satisfaction of growing a snake with smooth steering, moving prey, enemy snakes, boost management, and rare power-ups.

The game is currently in active development. Future plans include more arena themes, additional creatures and power-ups, expanded settings, accessibility improvements, and an optional leaderboard. Features may change as the game is tested and improved.

FAQ

Good things to know

Is Slither N' Slash free?

Yes. The game is free to play in your browser and does not require an account.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Phones and tablets use an on-screen movement pad and boost control. For the best experience, rotate a smaller phone if you need more arena space.

Can I save my progress?

The game saves your high score in local storage on your current browser. Runs and high scores do not sync between devices.

How do power-ups work?

Rare targets appear during longer runs. Catching a fireball activates Berserker mode for a limited time, while rare prey can award extra points.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Slither N' Slash currently has no user accounts and does not ask for personal information to play.
